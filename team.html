Our Team

The people behind Certes Institute's research and analysis.

Leadership

Pip Foweraker

Executive Director, Research

Pip Foweraker is the founder of Certes, building durable infrastructure and support for AI safety researchers and other work on catastrophic and existential risk. Drawing on experience in operations and governance across finance, government, and nonprofits, including serving as COO of Ashgro, Pip is presently working on establishing operations and hiring for Certes' 2026 research agenda.

Consulting Experts

Charlie Renner

Food Resilience & Disaster Preparedness

Charlie Renner works with Certes on food resilience and community disaster preparedness, drawing on his role as Food Resilience Officer with Far South Food Resilience in southern Tasmania. Deeply embedded in local networks around Huonville and the far south, Charlie helps design and deliver practical projects that strengthen regional self-reliance before and during crises.
