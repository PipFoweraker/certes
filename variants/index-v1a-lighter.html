What We Do

Bringing clarity and rigour to the assessment of global catastrophic risks

⚖

Threat Evaluation

Systematic analysis of catastrophic and existential risks, from artificial intelligence to biosecurity, climate disruption to nuclear threats. We assess likelihood, severity, and intervention points.

⚙

Strategic Guidance

Evidence-based recommendations for policymakers, institutions, and researchers. We translate complex risk landscapes into actionable strategic frameworks.

Research & Analysis

Original research on risk evaluation methodologies, intervention effectiveness, and governance mechanisms. We publish openly to advance the field.

Our Approach

Certes brings the methodical rigour of traditional risk assessment to novel and unprecedented challenges. We combine deep technical expertise with strategic clarity, providing analysis that is both scientifically grounded and practically actionable.

We work with governments, research institutions, and international bodies to ensure that catastrophic risks receive the serious, sustained attention they require.
