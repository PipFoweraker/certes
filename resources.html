Resources

Organizations and institutions working on catastrophic and existential risk

Research Organizations

  • Center for AI Safety
    Nonprofit research organization working to reduce societal-scale risks from artificial intelligence.
  • Centre for the Study of Existential Risk
    Cambridge University research center studying risks that could lead to human extinction or civilizational collapse.
  • Center for Security and Emerging Technology
    Policy research organization analyzing the security impacts of emerging technologies at Georgetown University.
  • Johns Hopkins Center for Health Security
    Leading center for biosecurity research, focusing on pandemic preparedness and biological threats.
  • Nuclear Threat Initiative
    Nonprofit working to prevent catastrophic attacks with weapons of mass destruction and disruption.

Funding Organizations

  • Open Philanthropy
    Grantmaking organization focused on high-impact giving, including significant work on existential risk.
  • Survival and Flourishing Fund
    Fund supporting work that improves humanity's long-term prospects for survival and flourishing.
  • Long-Term Future Fund
    Grantmaking fund supporting projects that improve the long-term trajectory of civilization.

Policy & Governance

  • AI Safety Institute
    UK government organization focused on advanced AI safety research and evaluation.
  • UN Office for Disaster Risk Reduction
    United Nations body focused on disaster risk reduction and resilience building.

Additional Resources

  • AISafety.info
    Comprehensive resource on AI safety research, maintained by a global team of specialists and volunteers.
